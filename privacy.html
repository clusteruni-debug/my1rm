Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

My1RM is a static MVP. The calculator runs in your browser and does not require an account. Lift inputs are not sent to a database by this version.

Local privacy preferences

If you choose a privacy option on the site, My1RM stores that preference in your browser's localStorage so the banner does not need to appear on every visit. This preference is not sent to a database by the MVP.

Location

When hosted on Cloudflare Pages, My1RM can call a lightweight location endpoint that reads Cloudflare request metadata such as country, region, city, timezone, and data center code. This is used only to label the current result as a coarse estimate. The location endpoint is called only after you choose to allow the location label. The MVP does not store IP addresses or create a local leaderboard.

Cookies

The MVP does not set advertising cookies because Google AdSense has not been enabled yet. If AdSense is enabled later, Google and other third-party vendors may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or other identifiers to serve and measure ads. Users may opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ads Settings.

Advertising

This page should be updated with the final AdSense publisher details before ads go live. For regions where consent is legally required, configure AdSense Privacy & messaging or another compliant consent-management tool before serving personalized ads.
