Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-06-09

My1RM runs in your browser and does not require an account. The calculator itself works entirely on your device. If you choose to see your rank, one anonymous record is saved — see "Ranking records" below.

Local privacy preferences

If you choose a privacy option on the site, My1RM stores that preference in your browser's localStorage so the banner does not need to appear on every visit. This preference is not sent to a database by the MVP.

Ranking records

If you tap "See my rank", My1RM saves one anonymous record: your estimated squat, bench, deadlift, and total one-rep max, your selected sex and age band, and a coarse region (city and country) derived from your connection. No name, email, account, or raw IP address is stored — the region is read from Cloudflare's edge metadata and the IP itself is discarded. These records are only used to compute the "top %" and city standings shown back to visitors.

Location

My1RM can call a lightweight location endpoint that reads Cloudflare request metadata such as country, region, and city to label your result as a coarse estimate. The raw IP address is never stored.

Cookies

The MVP does not set advertising cookies because Google AdSense has not been enabled yet. If AdSense is enabled later, Google and other third-party vendors may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or other identifiers to serve and measure ads. Users may opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ads Settings.
