Terms and safety disclaimer

My1RM provides estimated training numbers for informational use. It is not medical advice, coaching advice, competition judging, or a substitute for professional supervision.

Use at your own risk

One-rep max attempts can be risky. Warm up properly, use spotters or safety equipment, and do not attempt a max because a calculator suggested it.

Benchmark limitations

The percentile display is a demo model. It is designed to make the MVP useful and transparent, not to certify a true city, national, or federation rank.

No account service

This version does not provide user accounts, saved records, or official leaderboards.
